InfiniTicTacToe - Infinite Tic-Tac-Toe Game

This is a game of tic-tac-toe, but it is played on an infinite board. The goal is to get 5 in a row, but the board is infinite, so you can play in any direction. The game is played by two players, who take turns placing their mark on the board. When a player gets 5 in a row, they get a point and the game is continued. Crossed out cells are not allowed to be played again.
